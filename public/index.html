▸ How to deploy your NFT collection

        

          
1

          
Connect your EVM wallet (MetaMask, Rabby, WalletConnect) using the wallet section above.

        

        

          
2

          
Select the target blockchain from the network dropdown. Make sure your wallet is on the matching network.

        

        

          
3

          
Fill in your collection name, symbol, max supply, and mint price. Set max per wallet limit.

        

        

          
4

          
Enter your Hidden URI (placeholder metadata shown before reveal) and Base URI (real metadata URL).

        

        

          
5

          
Click Deploy Contract and review the transaction preview. Confirm in your wallet to deploy.

        

        

          
6

          
After deployment, save your contract address. Use the Reveal tab to reveal your collection on OpenSea.

▸ The Full Reveal Flow

        

          
1

          
Deploy your ERC721 contract on the Deploy tab. Set a Hidden URI (placeholder metadata) and a Base URI (real metadata).

        

        

          
2

          
Mint — users mint NFTs. They see only the placeholder art (hidden metadata).

        

        

          
3

          
Reveal — come to this tab and call reveal() to switch from hidden to real metadata. Only the contract deployer can do this.

        

        

          
4

          
Refresh — go to the Refresh tab to trigger OpenSea metadata refresh so collectors see the real artwork.

        

      


      

        
▸ How to reveal your collection

        

          
1

          
Connect the wallet that deployed the contract. Only the contract owner can reveal.

        

        

          
2

          
Select the same network where your contract is deployed from the dropdown.

        

        

          
3

          
Enter your contract address (0x...) and click Read Contract Status to verify current state.

        

        

          
4

          
Enter the new Base URI — this should be the IPFS URL or HTTPS URL pointing to your real metadata files (e.g., ipfs://QmXXX/).

        

        

          
5

          
Optionally validate metadata URLs by clicking Validate Metadata URLs to check if token metadata files are accessible.

        

        

          
6

          
Click Confirm Reveal and approve the transaction in your wallet. This sets the real baseURI and marks the collection as revealed.

        

        

          
7

          
After reveal, go to the Refresh tab to refresh metadata on OpenSea so collectors see the real artwork.

▸ How to refresh metadata on OpenSea

        

          
1

          
Paste your OpenSea collection URL in the field above and click Parse Collection.

        

        

          
2

          
Click the OpenSea link to open your collection page. If your collection was recently revealed, the images may still show placeholder art.

        

        

          
3

          
On the OpenSea collection page, click the ••• (more options) menu in the top-right area.

        

        

          
4

          
Select Refresh metadata from the dropdown menu. OpenSea will queue a metadata refresh for your collection.

        

        

          
5

          
Wait approximately 5-10 minutes for the refresh to complete. You can check back by viewing individual token pages.

        

        

          
6

          
For individual token refresh, visit the token page on OpenSea, click the ••• menu, and select Refresh metadata.
